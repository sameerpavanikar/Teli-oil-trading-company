Soyabee Oil

Soyabee oil is a type of vegetable oil that is extracted from soybeans. It is commonly used for cooking and frying due to its high smoke point and mild flavor. Soyabee oil is rich in polyunsaturated fats, which are considered heart-healthy fats. It also contains vitamin E, an antioxidant that helps protect the body from damage caused by free radicals. Soyabee oil can be used in various culinary applications, including sautéing, deep-frying, and salad dressings.
