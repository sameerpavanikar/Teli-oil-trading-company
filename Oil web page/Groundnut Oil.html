Groundnut oil

Groundnut oil, also known as peanut oil, is a type of vegetable oil that is extracted from peanuts. It is commonly used for cooking and frying due to its high smoke point and mild flavor. Groundnut oil is rich in monounsaturated fats, which are considered heart-healthy fats. It also contains vitamin E, an antioxidant that helps protect the body from damage caused by free radicals. Groundnut oil can be used in various culinary applications, including sautéing, deep-frying, and salad dressings.
